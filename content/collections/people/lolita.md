short_bio: 'Lolita lives in La Salle, outside Courmayeur in the Val d’Aosta. She was born and raised in Todi in southern Umbria. After finishing school, she studied natural sciences at University of Perugia and graduated with a PhD in biology.  She worked in research and environmental education until she moved to the Val d’Aosta 2007.  In 2018, she became an environmental hiking guide and has combined this with her research and teaching responsibilities.  Lolita is a qualified AIGEA hiking guide and, since 2022, she has been the Vice President of the Italian Association of Environmental Hiking Guides.'
