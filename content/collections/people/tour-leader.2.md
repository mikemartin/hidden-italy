short_bio: 'Simon first went to Italy as a backpacker in 1980 and fell in love with the country. He returned in 1983,  living and working in Milan til 1988. He then worked at  UNSW (Sydney), taking guided tours to Italy annually. He spent two years with his family in Prato (Florence) in 2003 and 2004, working at the Monash University Centre. Since then he has dedicated himself full-time to Hidden Italy. He had articles and photographs on Italy published in most major Australian newspapers and magazines.   His book Trails to Freedom was published in  2025, about a solo hike he did across the Italian Alps.'
