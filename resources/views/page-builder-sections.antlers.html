{{# How it works #}}
{{ partial:typography/h2 content="How sections are defined" }}

The main page builder is a single replicator field defined in resources/fieldsets/page_builder.yaml. It is imported into the page, home and product blueprints. Sets are organised into three groups: Image and text, Interactive and Marketing sections. Each set imports its own fieldset and renders through a partial in resources/views/page_builder/.

Tour-specific sections live inside the guided/tour.yaml and self-guided/tour.yaml blueprints as inline replicator sets. They have view partials but no separate fieldsets, which is why their fields can drift between blueprint files.
